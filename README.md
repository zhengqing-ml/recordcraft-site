## 🛡️ Privacy Policy

### Effective Date and Scope
Effective date: March 13, 2026.

This Privacy Policy applies to the current RecordCraft app and explains how the app handles information on your device and through Apple platform services. It covers RecordCraft itself. The App Store, Apple system services, and any third-party model host or destination you choose for export may have their own privacy terms.

### Information RecordCraft Processes
RecordCraft may process audio you record, audio or video files you import, transcripts, speaker labels, summaries, titles, folder organization, timestamps, durations, language selections, downloaded model assets, app settings, and purchase entitlement status. For imported video files, RecordCraft is designed to keep the audio track and does not need the video track for its core workflows.

If you enable location, RecordCraft may store location metadata with a recording. If you do not enable location, RecordCraft should not attach location metadata to new recordings.

On macOS, if you enable Meeting Detection, RecordCraft may locally check launches or activations of supported meeting apps so it can offer a recording prompt.

On macOS, if you choose system audio capture, RecordCraft uses Screen Recording permission to access system audio. The current app implementation discards screen video frames and is designed to keep audio only.

### How Data Is Stored
RecordCraft is designed for on-device use. In the current app version, recordings, transcripts, summaries, manifests, waveforms, and app settings are stored locally in the app's container on your device. RecordCraft does not require you to create a user account and does not routinely upload your recordings or summaries to developer-operated servers.

Exported files are copied only when you choose to export or share them. Files you export may then be stored by the destination you choose, such as Files, Finder, AirDrop, Mail, or another share target.

### Permissions and Device Access
Depending on the feature you use, RecordCraft may ask for access to the microphone, speech recognition, location, notifications, Screen Recording on macOS for system audio capture, and user-selected files for import or export.

These permissions are used to provide the related feature. You can deny or later change permissions in system settings, but some app functions may then be limited or unavailable.

### When Information May Leave Your Device
Some information may be sent off device only in limited cases tied to platform or user actions.

When you make or restore an in-app purchase, Apple processes the transaction. RecordCraft may receive transaction status and entitlement information from StoreKit, but Apple generally handles billing and payment details.

When you choose to download optional model assets, your device contacts model-hosting providers integrated with the app. Whisper transcription models are downloaded from the argmaxinc/whisperkit-coreml repository on Hugging Face (operated by argmax, inc.). Gemma 3 summary models are downloaded from mlx-community/gemma-3-4b-it-qat-4bit or mlx-community/gemma-3-12b-it-qat-4bit on Hugging Face, provided by mlx-community and subject to the Gemma Terms of Use. FluidAudio speaker-identification and voice-activity-detection models may also be sourced from FluidInference repositories on Hugging Face. A Hugging Face mirror (hf-mirror.com) may be used as a fallback. Those providers may receive technical request data such as IP address, device or app version details, and download logs under their own policies.

Some model assets, including speaker-diarization, voice-activity-detection models, and optionally the Gemma 3 4B summary model, may be bundled directly within the RecordCraft app package and installed locally on first launch without a network request.

When you export, share, or open content with another app or service, the selected destination will receive the content you choose to send.

### How RecordCraft Uses Information
RecordCraft uses processed information to provide audio recording, import, playback, transcription, speaker identification, summary generation, search, organization, export, and purchase-access control inside the app.

As of this app version, RecordCraft does not include advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking, or routine third-party analytics based on the code currently implemented.

### AI Outputs and Sensitive Information
Because transcripts, speaker labels, summaries, titles, and other outputs are generated from source recordings and automated models, they may contain personal, sensitive, confidential, inaccurate, offensive, or other objectionable information derived from the source material or inferred by the model.

Please review output carefully before relying on it or exporting, publishing, sharing, or using it with any third party.

### Retention and Deletion
RecordCraft keeps local data until you delete it, remove the app, or clear it through device management tools. Items moved to Trash may remain on your device until they are permanently deleted from within the app.

Permanent deletion is intended to remove the local app copy, but copies you exported, shared, or included in system backups may still exist outside RecordCraft.

### Children's Privacy
RecordCraft is not designed as a children's service. Do not use RecordCraft to collect or process another person's voice, location, or other personal information unless you have the legal right and any required consent to do so.

### Changes and Contact
RecordCraft may update this Privacy Policy as features, platform requirements, or legal obligations change. Material updates should appear in a later app version or published policy update.

## ⚙️ Privacy Choices

RecordCraft is designed for on-device use, meaning you possess absolute control over your recordings and data locally on your device.

Because recordings, transcripts, summaries, and app settings are stored locally in the app's container on your device (and not routinely uploaded to developer-operated servers), there is no centralized remote account to delete.
- **To delete your data entirely**, simply remove or uninstall the app from your device, and permanently delete any items you moved to the Trash within the app.
- **System Backups:** If you utilize iCloud or system backups, copies included in backups may still exist outside RecordCraft until those backups are managed or deleted via Apple's device management tools.
